THRONE ROOM
10th floor

The Throne was an area designed by the guild, Ainz Ooal Gown to welcome invaders who managed to penetrate the defense of the dungeon. There the guild members would assemble here for a final showdown. The Overseer Guardian Albedo was the only NPC allowed to be stationed here, acting as the overall commander to the Floor Guardians.

Sadly no players had ever reached the Throne Room, not even the Union of Eight Guilds when they launched their invasion of Nazarick, making the chamber little more than a decoration.
